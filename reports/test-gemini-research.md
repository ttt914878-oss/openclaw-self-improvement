### 3-Bullet Summary
*   **Fundamental Shift in Processing:** Unlike classical computers that use binary bits (0 or 1), quantum computers utilize **qubits**, which can exist in a state of superposition (representing both 0 and 1 simultaneously).
*   **Exponential Power:** Leveraging quantum mechanical phenomena like **entanglement** and interference allows these systems to perform massive parallel computations, solving specific complex problems exponentially faster than supercomputers.
*   **Specialized Applications:** This technology is not for general-purpose computing but is targeted at high-complexity fields such as **molecular simulation (drug discovery)**, cryptographic breaking, financial modeling, and logistics optimization.
